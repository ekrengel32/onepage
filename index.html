HELLO

I am a student of design and architecture living in Chicago, but I consider myself a mindful observer. I want to become a licensed architect because I want to manipulate space and I believe architecture studies the spatial relationships between humans and environments. As an architect, I plan to establish a mindful approach that focuses on site specific and client oriented designs, while being open to any type of building use. I believe it is my responsibility to manipulate space and create environments for human engagement.

EXPERIENCE

I worked as a building designer for a Design/Build firm in Austin, Texas. There are four homes either built or currently under construction that my team put together with full attention to client and site needs. Additionally, I worked as a CAD expeditor for various clients in the city of Austin. One of my clients was the mayor of New Braunfels, Texas. I developed a renovation plan for a culinary arts kitchen in an old kayak and tubing center for the Guadalupe River, just outside of downtown New Braunfels.

EDUCATION

I currently study architecture at Illinois Institute of Technology (IIT) because of the rich architectural history associated with the school and the city of Chicago. I also believe the faculty offers a multitude of backgrounds from long-tenured professors to practicing architects who can provide different perspectives for my education. The facilities are condusive for aspiring designers, which includes a large workshop equipped with large and small tools that facilitate many design and build scenarios.
